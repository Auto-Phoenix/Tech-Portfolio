🎬
FILM & TV DIVISION
HEAD OF FILM · REPORTS TO CEO
Original series, films, documentaries, and music videos produced in-house. Sky Studios content distributed to African and global streaming platforms.

Dance, theatre, and live performance. Manages Sky Academy, talent development, and performing arts programming. Backbone of Sky's live event productions.
🏆
EVENTS & AWARDS
HEAD OF EVENTS · REPORTS TO COO
Annual Sky Awards ceremony, concert programming, red carpet events, and private bookings. The Grand Hall is the department's primary stage.
🌐
TECHNOLOGY & DIGITAL
CTO · REPORTS TO CEO
Sky's streaming platform, broadcast infrastructure, cybersecurity, and tech innovation. Manages all studio systems, MCR, and digital content pipelines.
📣
MARKETING & BRAND
CCO · REPORTS TO CEO
Sky's identity, artist branding, campaigns, social media, PR, and partnerships with global brands. Custodians of the Sky Enterprise image across Africa and beyond.
⚖️
LEGAL & FINANCE
CFO + GENERAL COUNSEL · C-SUITE
Contracts, intellectual property, royalties, investor relations, and financial governance. The backbone that protects Sky Enterprise's assets and ensures artist rights are honoured.
